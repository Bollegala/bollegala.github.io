Danushka Bollegala

Professor@UoL, Amazon Scholar@Amazon
I am Danushka Bollegala, Professor in Natural Language Processing (NLP) at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Liverpool, United Kingdom. I lead the Machine Learning (ML) and NLP Research Groups. I am also an Amazon Scholar working at the cutting-edge information retrieval problems at Amazon Search. I am amazed by the human's ability to understand and process languages and interested in developing algorithms to teach similar skills to machines.

Works

Deep Learning Text Book

This is a one of the pioneering and best selling textbooks written in Japanese on Deep Learning. It covers both theoretical fundamentals of Deep Learning as well as its applications to various domains such as text and vision.

Machine Learning on Web Data

This book, written in Japanse, explains the NLP techniques that are applicable on Web text mining. It is written for Web engineers.

Detail
Attributional and Relational Similarity on the Web

This is the book covering my PhD thesis, where I studied methods for estimating attributional and relational similarity between words using information available on the Web.
